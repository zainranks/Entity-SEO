# Page decision prompt

Decide whether an entity should receive a standalone URL, a section, a mention, or rejection.

Evaluate:
- distinct search intent or user task
- business and conversion relevance
- unique information depth
- relationship to a parent entity
- overlap with planned pages
- evidence quality and maintenance value
- compliance and accuracy risk

Return: decision, recommended page type, parent page, primary intent, rationale, required supporting entities, overlap risks, and confidence.

Never recommend a standalone page solely because an entity is frequent or appears in competitors.
